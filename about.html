Johnny Nava was born on April 5, 1993 in San Francisco, California. He began writing his senior year of high school, and began competing in contests and writing for a variety of popular podcasts shortly after. When he is not writing in third person, Johnny enjoys filmmaking, playing guitar, exercising, procrastinating, and writing stories, essays and screenplays. He currently resides in Los Angeles, California.

If you would like to get in touch, please visit the contact page listed above. In the meantime, here are some fun facts.

1. Despite being as white as a late-stage Michael Jackson, I am actually half Mexican. I have recently come to terms with being referred to as the “Pale Mexican” at family parties.

2. In college, my friends and I shot a wrestling movie in my backyard. In between takes we entertained ourselves by recording ourselves rapping. Unfortunately, those raps still exist.

3. I once met a group of Malaysians in Rome who decided to sponsor my drinking for the night. The next day I woke up, fully clothed, in a bathtub near the Coliseum.
