Hello, it's me: that guy above: Johnny Nava (obviously.) If you clicked this page I imagine it's because you want to know more about me so here are the basics: I am 24 years old, I live in Los Angeles, California, and when I am not procrastinating, my main hobbies include exercising and writing.

I began reading as a hobby near the tail of high school, but found it difficult to identify with a many of the themes and perspectives presented by contemporary literature. So I decided to contribute to it. It started with short stories, then writing contests, then podcasts, and eventually evolved into an obsession the result of which is the site you see before you today.
