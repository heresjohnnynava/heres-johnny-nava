Johnny Nava was born on April 5, 1993 in San Francisco, California. He began writing his senior year of high school, and began competing in contests and writing for a variety of popular podcasts shortly after. When he is not writing in third person, Johnny enjoys filmmaking, playing guitar, exercising, and writing stories, essays, and screenplays. He currently resides in Los Angeles, California.
